🛢️ WordQuest: Barrel Blitz

Shoot the correct barrel before it lands. Wrong barrel = poison, lose a life. If a barrel actually reaches your hero without being shot, that costs a life too — barrels that fall past him are safe misses.

A quick round of questions figures out the best level for you, then you jump straight in.

❓ How to Play

🎯 The goal: answer each question correctly before it's too late.

🛢️ Click Mode: barrels fall, each showing a different answer. Click the barrel that answers the clue at the top.

⌨️ Typed Mode: sometimes a "?" mystery barrel falls instead — type your answer in the box and press Go! (or hit Enter). You can try again if you get it wrong, as long as the barrel hasn't landed yet.

☠️ Wrong answer: costs a life — the barrel turns to poison.

🎯 Barrel reaches your hero: also costs a life — don't let one slip past uncaught!

❤️ Lives: you start with 3. Game over when they run out.

🏆 Level Complete: get 10 correct answers to clear a level and move on.

🧭 Not sure where to start? Try Quest mode from the main menu — a quick round of questions finds the right level for you.

⏸️ Need a break? Use the Pause button anytime during a level.
